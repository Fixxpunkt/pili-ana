The role of news media

One of the key functions of journalism and news media is to enable an informed public discourse on public affairs, politics and policy. Next to the traditional news reporting and analysis, digital media has found new ways for the public to take part in this discourse: the comments sections on news platforms. Available on most news websites and apps, read by a significant part of most publications audiences, and ferociously contributed to by a very active core user base, they are one of the most underestimated factors in how public opinion is formed.

The vast amount of comments submitted by users, the often unrestrained hate and bile, spambots and lunatics, cynical and derogatory language, racism, sexism, off-topic reactions, and blatant nonsense, make moderation of user comments one of the most daunting tasks of modern day publishing.

Most media have staffed own community management teams to take care of comments. This is obviously efficient, as journalists and editors free up time to do original reporting, but it is also a missed opportunity. If journalists and editors were to take part in the discussions on their reporting, they could immediately react to the most important comments. For often, there are a few key comments to each piece of content, such as questions, arguments or opinions, that have a big impact on how a story is perceived by the reader.

Taking back the initiative

When trolls overrun comment sections, when fake news seem just as trustworthy as original reporting, media are failing and democracy is in danger. It is essential that journalists take the initiative and enter into discussion with their audience. They need to answer questions directly, challenge alternative facts and help the public discourse along the way. Pili-ana is an open-source, mobile-friendly web application to help moderating online discussions and comments. The mobile-friendly GUI allows journalists to efficiently approve, delete and reply to comments.

Benefits

Mobile-friendly and open-source

Pili-ana is very easy to integrate in any existing system. Only embed the Javascript and provide the API endpoints and you are ready to go.

Swipe functionality to fast and easily approve or delete comments

Simply swipe left to delete a comment or right to approve it. As easy as that, just like Tinder.

Showing the toxicity level of a comment

Pili-ana shows the toxicity score calculated by Jigsaw's Perspective API to help the moderator to approve or delete a comment.
